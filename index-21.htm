Welcome to Reel Classics, the Internet's most comprehensive site dedicated exclusively to Classic Movies.  Comprising over 2500 pages and more than 3 gigabytes of content (with much more on the way), it may well be the biggest too.  Enjoy your look around and remember to come back soon.  Reel Classics is constantly being revised and updated.

Main Sections:

The Stars
Individual pages about hundreds of Actors, Actresses, Animation Voices and Screen Teams -- the most well developed and popular section of Reel Classics.
The Films
Individual pages on a number of classic Movies and Musicals as well as some favorite film Series.
The Movie Makers
Pages on individual Directors, Producers, Studios, Costume Designers, Musicians, Production Designers, Cinematographers, Choreographers and other Artisans who made it happen behind the scenes.

Audio/Video Gallery
A collection of classic movie music files, quotes, and film clips for your listening and viewing pleasure.
Merchandise
A highly organized section of links to dealers who sell almost anything related to classic movies, from videos, DVDs and posters to books, autographs, photographs and other memorabilia.
Commentary & Reviews
Elizabeth's opinions galore, including her favorites, films she recommends, as well as her reviews.
Articles
Lots of classic movie-related articles, both new and vintage, covering subjects ranging from specific films to the classic Hollywood era.
Features
Including the Trivia Contest, listings of Celebrity Addresses and Birthdays, Classic Wallpapers, Screensavers and Desktop Themes as well as Classic Movie News Briefs.
Links
Links to some of the greatest classic movie sites on the Internet -- and just about every other classic movie-related site too.  Also, places to see classics on the big screen.

Site Specifics
Information about the Reel Classics website, including sections for What's New, Awards, Acknowledgements, my Author's Notes and the Reel Classics Newsletter.
About the Author
A little background on Elizabeth, the author of Reel Classics, including where she came from and how she got involved in all this.
Feedback & Questions
A place for you to submit your comments, suggestions, and yes, even questions about Reel Classics or just classic movies in general.
Search
A simple search engine to help you locate the classic movie subject you're looking for.
