NBFF 2020

For Film Fans

A Unique Experience for All Film Fans

As the West Coast’s fastest-growing film festival, NBFF offers films from 50 countries across multiple genres. In addition to our Narrative and Documentary programs, NBFF carefully curates a collection of short film programs and our interest programs include Action Sports, Art, Architecture + Design, Culinary, Family, and Horror films.

View 2020 Program

For Sponsors

Opportunities for Brand Exposure & Entertainment

NBFF offers custom experiential and advertising opportunities for brands looking to reach over 50,000 culturally active Southern Californians.

We also offer private screenings and events to discounted hotel rates, leisure activities, and sponsor screening passes, the NBFF team will work with you to create the ideal setting for your next corporate retreat or client entertainment event.

Get to Newport Beach

John Wayne Airport (SNA)

John Wayne Airport, Orange County (SNA) is approximately 7 miles from our main venues and hotel at Fashion Island.

Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)

LAX is the largest and busiest airport in  the state of California and is approximately 40 miles north of Newport Beach.

About NBFF

For 21 years, the Newport Beach Film Festival has strived to connect our Orange County audience with the best in independent and international films while hosting events where the Newport Beach and filmmaking communities can gather to enjoy tastings and hosted cocktails from the area’s best culinary and beverage partners.
